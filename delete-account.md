Account Deletion Request - The Shabbat App
We respect your right to privacy and data control. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

1. How to Delete Your Account

In-App: Go to Settings > Account and select "Delete Account." This will immediately remove your profile, name, and zip code from our database.

Via Email: If you no longer have access to the app, email us at [Your-Email] with the subject line "Account Deletion Request."

2. Data That Will Be Deleted
Upon deletion, the following will be permanently removed from our records:

Your Name (used for greetings and dedications).

Your Zip Code (used for candle lighting times).

Your personalized Menu Planner checklists.

3. Data We Retain
We do not retain any personal data after account deletion. Subscription billing records are handled by Google Play and may be retained by them for financial record-keeping as per their own policies.
